About the farm

The J. Steckle Heritage Homestead not-forprofit organization was established in 1988 by Dr. Jean Steckle who donated the land and facilities to preserve the site as an education centre for the youth of our community. We have education programs that are attended by primary schools throughout the region, a Junior Leader program for teenagers, and summer camps where kids get the chance to learn about farm life and healthy food.
